Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of the world's great dishes. Hailing from the rolling hills of Italy, the humble lasagna is a veritable cornucopia of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce. Try it! You won't be disappointed.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Bolognese sauce
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Make your bolognese sauce
  2. Layer pasta, sauce, and cheese
  3. Repeat layering until all ingredients are used
  4. Bake at 400 for 45 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
