Manhattan

Description

Ah, the Manhattan, the most classic of cocktails. Boozy but balanced, it is an elegant drink for any occassion. Rye whiskey melds with sweet vermouth, tied together with Angostura bitters and a brandied cherry. Can life get any sweeter?

Ingredients

  • 2 oz rye whiskey
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashed Angostura bitters

Steps

  1. Combine whiskey, vermouth, and bitters in a mixing glass
  2. Add ice and stire
  3. Strain into a chilled coupe glass
  4. Garnish with brandied cherry
  5. Enjoy!
